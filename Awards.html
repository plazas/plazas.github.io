Honors & Awards

Zaccheus Daniel Foundation for Astronomical Science Award
University of Pennsylvania
Awarded for excellence in astronomical research and contributions to the field of physics and astronomy during my doctoral studies.

Fundación Alejandro Ángel Escobar National Prize in Natural and Exact Sciences
Colombia
Recognized as the highest scientific honor in Colombia, awarded for my significant contributions to the understanding and characterization of systematic errors in weak gravitational lensing.

NASA Hubble Postdoctoral Fellowship
California Institute of Technology, NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory
Selected for my research on systematic errors in weak lensing from the infrared detectors used by the Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope.

Dark Energy Survey Collaboration Awards
Awarded for my work on weak gravitational lensing and the testing of the Dark Energy Camera detectors at Fermilab, as part of the Dark Energy Survey project.
