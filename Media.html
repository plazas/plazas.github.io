Media and Press

Featured Articles

My work and contributions to astrophysics and outreach have been highlighted in various media outlets. Below are some selected articles and interviews:

  • Dark Energy Survey: Scientist of the Week - A feature on my contributions to the Dark Energy Survey, focusing on my work in weak gravitational lensing and systematic error characterization.
  • Big Astronomy: Real People, Big Astronomy - An interview discussing my journey in astronomy and my role in various significant projects, including the Rubin Observatory and the Roman Space Telescope.
  • Harvard Gazette: An Evening of Stars, Solar Flares, and Agujeros Negros - Coverage of a public event I co-organized, highlighting the importance of bilingual outreach in astronomy.
  • Tower Grove Park: Astronomy Festival - Information about the first-ever Astronomy Festival at Tower Grove Park, an event I proposed and co-organized to bring the wonders of the universe to the local community.
  • Princeton University: Astronomy on Tap in Trenton - An article discussing the Astronomy on Tap series I founded in Trenton, NJ, which brings astrophysics to the public in a casual setting.

Podcasts and Interviews

I have also been featured in several podcasts where I discuss various aspects of my work, science communication, and the importance of diversity in STEM fields:

  • Visión Cósmica - As the creator and co-host of this Spanish-language podcast, I discuss the latest in astronomy and space science with a focus on reaching Spanish-speaking audiences worldwide.

Public Events

I am actively involved in organizing and participating in public outreach events. Here are some of the key events I’ve contributed to:

  • Astronomy Festival at Tower Grove Park - Bringing together experts and the public for a night of stargazing, talks, and interactive activities.
  • An Evening of Stars, Solar Flares, and Agujeros Negros - A unique bilingual event held at Harvard, aimed at engaging diverse audiences in astronomy.
